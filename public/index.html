The format

Four contests. Enter any mix you like — they all settle into one running balance per person.

Your account

  • Sign up once with a username and password (top of the page). Every pick, point, and fee is tied to your account — no more guessing who "Pat M." is.
  • Your picks stay hidden from everyone else until each game kicks off.

Contest 1 — Bracket $40

  • Fill in the whole knockout bracket: pick the winner of every match from the Round of 32 to the Final.
  • You score points for each correct winner, escalating by round: R32 = 1, R16 = 2, QF = 4, SF = 8, Final = 16. Max 80.
  • A correct pick only counts if that team actually wins in that round — so a busted pick takes its branch down with it.
  • Bonus +5: exact final scoreline.
  • Bonus +5: correct Golden Boot (tournament top scorer) — settled at the end.
  • Locks at the first Round of 32 kickoff. Resubmit any time before then with the same display name to replace your bracket.

Contest 2 — Match picks $10

  • Predict the score of each knockout match.
  • Exact score = 3 pts. Correct result only (W/D/L) = 1 pt.
  • Scores are taken at the final whistle (after extra time; penalty shootouts don't change the goal tally).
  • Each match locks at its own kickoff. Predict as many or as few as you like; others' picks stay hidden until kickoff.

Contest 3 — Player to score $5 / pick

  • Pick one or more players you think will score in any given game. Totally optional — stack several players on one game, a few games, or none. Each player can only be picked once across the whole contest.
  • $5 per pick. For each of your players who scores in their game you get +2 points; if they don't, you get nothing. Either way each $5 is in the pot.
  • Goals in normal/extra time count (including penalties). Own goals and penalty-shootout goals don't count.
  • Runs on the current slate of games (group stage and knockouts) — it's live now, independent of the bracket. Each pick locks at that game's kickoff; remove a pick beforehand if you change your mind.
  • Graded automatically from the live feed. The pool admin can manually fix a pick if the feed can't resolve a name.

Bonus — Third-place playoff $20

  • Predict the score of the 3rd-place playoff (the two losing semifinalists).
  • Same scoring as match picks: exact = 3 pts, right result = 1 pt.
  • Winner takes all (ties split). Locks at that match's kickoff.

How to enter

  1. Sign up with a username and password at the top of the page (or log in).
  2. Go to Make your bracket, Make match picks, and/or Pick a scorer and make your picks. They land instantly — no Google Form.
  3. Come back any time before kickoff to update; your latest pick replaces the old one.

Payouts

  • Bracket: 1st 70% / 2nd 30%, 3rd gets their entry back (winner-take-all under 3 entries).
  • Match picks: 1st 70% / 2nd 30%, 3rd gets their entry back.
  • Player to score: own pot ($5 × every pick made), split 1st 70% / 2nd 30% / 3rd refund by total player-to-score points.
  • Third-place bonus: winner takes all.
  • The Results tab shows live projected payouts and the minimum set of transfers to settle up — all four contests netted into one balance per person.

Scores refresh automatically every few minutes from ESPN. No Google Forms, no spreadsheets.
